A Smart Companion for Connection

"Heartbeat" is a thoughtful and compassionate mobile application designed for caring individuals who want to stay connected with their loved ones. It provides a gentle, consistent safety net while fostering deeper relationships and peace of mind through personalized support, moving beyond simple check-ins to nurture meaningful connections.

Supporting Those Who Care

In a world where many live independently, caring individuals face unique challenges in staying connected with their loved ones. We aim to bridge the gap between caring hearts and those they love.

Worrying About Loved Ones

When family members live alone, caring individuals often worry about their well-being and safety, especially during times of illness or emergencies.

Staying Connected

Despite busy lives, caring people want to maintain meaningful connections with family and friends who live independently.

Peace of Mind

There's a need for a simple, non-intrusive way to check in on loved ones while respecting their independence and privacy.

Building Trust

Creating a safety net that builds trust and strengthens relationships rather than creating dependency or worry.

Growing Need for Connection

Smart Family Check-ins

The app sends caring, customizable reminders at times that work for families, prompting simple "I'm okay" responses that bring peace of mind to caring family members.

Family Alert System

If a loved one doesn't respond within a caring grace period, family members are gently notified, allowing them to reach out with love and support.

Family Connection Tools

Share meaningful moments, family photos, and caring messages to strengthen bonds and create lasting memories together.

Caring Conversation Starters

Receive gentle prompts to encourage meaningful family conversations, express love and gratitude, and deepen relationships.

Family Activity Suggestions

Discover activities that families can enjoy together, whether in person or virtually, strengthening bonds and creating shared experiences.

Well-being Support

With family consent, the app can suggest caring ways to support each other's well-being and mental health, fostering a culture of care.
